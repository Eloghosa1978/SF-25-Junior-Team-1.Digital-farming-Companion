Types of Farming

Crop Farming

Growing Crops like wheat, corn, and vegetables.

Livestock Farming

Raising animals like cows, chickens, and pigs.

Fun Fact: Some farms combine both crop and livestock farming!

Farming Tools

Tractor

Used for plowing and other heavy tasks.

Plow

Breaks up the soil for planting.

Hoe

Used for weeding and cultivating.

Fun Fact: Farmers use different tools for different jobs!

Daily Tasks on a Farm

Feeding Animals

Making sure animals have enough food and water.

Planting Seeds

Putting seeds in the ground to grow.

Harvesting Crops

Collecting ripe crops for use.

Fun Fact: Farmers work hard every day to keep the farm running!
