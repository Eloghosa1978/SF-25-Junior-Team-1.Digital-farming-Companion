What is Animal farming?

Animal farming (also called livestock farming) is all about raising animals for food, clothing, and other useful things. Farmers take care of animals like chickens, cows, goats, sheep, pigs, and even fish üê†!

Why is Animal Farming Important?

Food: We get eggs, milk, meat, and cheese from farm animals. Clothing: Wool from sheep and leather from cows help us make clothes and shoes. Fertilizer: Animal waste (called manure) helps crops grow better. Money: Farmers sell animals and animal products to earn income.
