🌱 What Is Green Smart Farming?

Green Smart Farming is the fusion of nature and technology. It’s how we grow more food with fewer resources, protect our environment, and create a smarter, sustainable future using drones, sensors, AI, and robotics.

🛠 Smart Tools We Use

Drones

Fly over farms to detect pests, diseases, and monitor growth.

Soil Sensors

Check soil health, moisture, and nutrient levels in real-time.

Farm Robots

Help with planting, spraying, and harvesting crops.

AI Predictions

Forecast best planting time using weather and data analysis.
