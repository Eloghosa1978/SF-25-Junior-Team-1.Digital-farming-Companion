Summary

Crops are special plants that farmers grow to give us food, clothes, or things we sell. They grow in soil with water and sunlight.

There are food crops we eat (like maize and beans), and cash crops that farmers sell to earn money (like cocoa and cotton).

Some crops love the rainy season, like rice and yams, while others grow better in the dry season with extra watering, like tomatoes and onions.

Crops have roots (to drink water), stems (to stand tall), leaves (to make food), and fruits or seeds (which we eat or plant again).

Farmers use simple tools like hoes and watering cans, or big machines like tractors to plant and care for their crops.

Yes, you can! You just need a cup, soil or cotton wool, a seed, and water. Place it in sunlight ‚Äî and your own crop will grow!
